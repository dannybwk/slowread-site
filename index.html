We scroll through hundreds of posts a day, yet struggle to finish a single book a year. We’ve lost the art of sitting still with words. SlowRead is built on a simple belief: a little reading, done consistently, changes everything. Not speed-reading. Not summaries. Just you, a passage, and the quiet space to think.
