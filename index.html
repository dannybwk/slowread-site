Previously on Pride and Prejudice

A wealthy bachelor, Mr. Bingley, has just moved to the neighbourhood. Mrs. Bennet is determined that one of her five daughters should marry him, and pressures her reluctant husband to pay a social call.

◆ Cultural Context

What is “Michaelmas”?

A Christian feast day on September 29th, widely used in Regency England to mark lease terms, harvest, and the start of autumn. In context: Bingley plans to move in before fall.
